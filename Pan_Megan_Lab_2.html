Introducing Degular!

Just a good regular degular typeface!

Degular is a Grotesque typeface created by OH no Type. While OH no is known for their fun and unique typography, Degular was designed to be the exact opposite.

Degular Website

OH no's website lets you try out the font with whatever text you want.

Characteristics of Degular

Degular, although aiming to be a regular degular typeface, still include a few aspects that add a little spice tand to accomodate for different optical sizes.

  • tall x-height
  • flat "g" ear
  • stroke contrast
  • tiny joints

Optical Sizes

Degular has 7 weights, each with its own italic.
To add on, there is Degular Text for text that is generally longer in length but smaller in size.
And finally, there is Degular Display for larger and bolder text.
